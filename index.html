About Me

Hey!? My name's Ahmed Gasim and I was born in Sudan but raised in Philladelphia since the age of 2. My journey as a front-end-developer started in 2014 while making a prank-site on Codeacademy and from there it sort of branched out into several sporadic attempts to conquer my terrible website styling. It wasn't until 2016 that I started digging into software programming languages like C and found myself cascading into the creative endeavor of front-end-development after struggling through the self-taught struggles of C.
